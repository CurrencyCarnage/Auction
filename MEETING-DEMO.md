## Suggested presentation flow

1. Open the link on the presentation screen.
2. Ask 2-4 people to open the same link from phones.
3. Give them different demo users.
4. Pick one lot, e.g. SHACMAN X3000.
5. One person places a normal bid.
6. Another person sets a proxy bid / “Bid up to”.
7. Another person places a higher bid and gets outbid by proxy.
8. Press Buy Now request and show that the auction stays live.
9. Show anonymous bid history.
10. Optional: use Reset demo before the official presentation starts.

## Important caveat

This is a fast presentation prototype with a shared backend. It is good for a meeting demo, not production. If this process/container stops, the links stop working.
